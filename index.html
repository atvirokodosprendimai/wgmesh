#features

Discover the core functionalities that make wgmesh stand out:

  • Secure End-to-End Encryption: Leveraging WireGuard for robust security.
  • Automatic Peer Discovery: Simplify network setup with intelligent discovery.
  • Cross-Platform Compatibility: Works on Linux, Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS.
  • High Performance: Optimized for speed and low latency.
  • Flexible Deployment: Deploy on cloud, bare metal, or edge devices.

#how-it-works

wgmesh creates a virtual overlay network that connects your devices directly, regardless of their physical location or underlying network topology. By using a secure mesh, data is routed efficiently and privately.

Learn more about the technical details in our README.

#install

Getting started with wgmesh is simple and straightforward. Follow our detailed installation guides for your preferred operating system or platform:

curl -sSfL https://install.wgmesh.dev | sh

Visit our GitHub repository for more installation options and advanced configurations.

#modes

wgmesh supports various operational modes to fit your specific needs:

  • Client Mode: Connect individual devices to an existing wgmesh network.
  • Server Mode: Establish a central hub for managing your mesh network.
  • Bridge Mode: Connect separate local networks into a unified mesh.
  • Anycast CDN Mode: Optimize content delivery with intelligent routing.

#story

The journey of wgmesh began with a vision to simplify secure, global connectivity. Born from the need for a resilient and decentralized networking solution, wgmesh evolved to provide robust, high-performance, and easy-to-manage mesh networks for individuals and enterprises alike.
